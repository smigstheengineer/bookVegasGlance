Through the years with Donny and Marie Osmond:
A Fan's Perspective

Edginess. Innovation. Artfulness.

Are there any better ways to describe Donny and Marie Osmond, their work and contribution to society? Embark on an epic journey of discovery, understanding and TRUTH while learning ALL you could possibly want to know about Donny and Marie Osmond and their work throughout the years – provided to you through the perspective of their most devoted fans.

A Fan's Perspective

This book will take you on a life long journey of fun, change, and courage. We explore truth and love while we share with you a true fan’s perspective of what it was like to follow Donny and Marie Osmond throughout the years. We see them develop, grow and evolve into the artists they are today. As teen idols, Donny and Marie Osmond have been striving to push the thresholds and move boundaries of the society. Were they successful at doing it? As highly forward-thinking, professional and committed group, they have always remained firmly committed to their mission of moving the threshold of what is perceived as “possible” and challenged the conventions related to culture, music and conventional thinking of that time.

About the Authors

Steve and Dawn Staton grew up in Michigan. Through life's twists and turns they ended up in Las Vegas, Nevada. Along their journey, they enjoyed a relationship with Donny and Marie Osmond that is shared through this book. "Through The Years With Donny and Marie Osmond" gives you a dovoted fans perspective. Having the Osmonds intertwined through life has enabled this journey. Steve and Dawn are grateful to Donny and Marie for making this story possible.
